Collectibles
Collectibles are items that are worth far more than their original sale price due to their rarity and/or popularity. Examples include art, antiques, coins, and stamps.

Common categories for classifying collectibles
    Category Description
    Art Paintings, sculptures, and other visual art pieces that are collected for their aesthetic value and historical significance.
    Antiques Items that are at least 100 years old and have historical or cultural significance.
    Coins Rare and valuable coins collected for their historical and monetary value.
    Stamps Rare and valuable postage stamps collected for their historical and philatelic value.
    Memorabilia Items related to famous people, events, or periods, such as sports memorabilia, movie props, and historical artifacts.
